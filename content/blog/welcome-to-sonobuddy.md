Whether you're a new grad walking into your first solo scan or an experienced sonographer who wants a faster reference than flipping through a textbook, SonoBuddy was built for you.

## What Is SonoBuddy?

SonoBuddy is a Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to give sonographers instant, phone-in-pocket access to the clinical information they need — right in the scan room.

No account required. No subscription wall. No loading spinner. Just open it and go.

## What's Inside

### Measurement Tables

Normal and abnormal reference values for:

- **Vascular** — carotid IMT, aorta, IVC, portal vein, renal artery doppler
- **Abdominal** — liver, spleen, kidneys, gallbladder, pancreas
- **OB/GYN** — uterus, ovaries, follicles, fetal biometry
- **Thyroid** — gland dimensions, nodule size thresholds
- **Cardiac** — basic echo measurements

Every measurement includes a clinical note so you understand *why* the threshold matters, not just what the number is.

### Exam Protocols

Step-by-step scanning guides with probe selection tips, key images to capture for PACS, and a full report checklist. Protocols currently include:

- Carotid duplex
- Lower extremity venous DVT survey
- Abdominal aorta
- Renal artery doppler
- Abdominal complete
- OB first trimester

### Clinical Calculators

Built-in calculators that return instant, color-coded results:

- **ABI** (Ankle-Brachial Index) with PAD severity grading
- **Resistive Index** with renal interpretation
- **Carotid Stenosis** grading (SRU 2003 criteria)
- **Organ Volume** (ellipsoid formula)
- **AFI** (Amniotic Fluid Index)
- **Gestational Age & EDD** from LMP or CRL
- **Thyroid Volume**

### Pathology Library

Concise cards for the most common pathologies you'll encounter — ultrasound findings, red flags, differentials, and practical reporting tips.

## Why We Built It

New sonography graduates told us the same thing over and over: *"I keep a stack of papers in my pocket because I can't find anything fast enough mid-scan."*

Existing apps either cost money, require an account, or are built for physicians rather than the person holding the probe. SonoBuddy is purpose-built for sonographers, with a UI designed for one-handed use in a darkened scan room.
